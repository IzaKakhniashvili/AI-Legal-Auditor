Software Development Agreement
Contract ID: CNT-2025-0147
Date: March 15, 2025
Between: Meridian Holdings Group ("Client") and ByteForge Solutions Ltd ("Developer")
1. Engagement Overview
The Client engages the Developer to design, develop, and deploy a custom Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
system. The Developer shall provide all necessary personnel, tools, and infrastructure to complete the project according to the
specifications set forth in this agreement.
2. Scope of Work
2.1 Project Description
The Developer shall build a cloud-based CRM system with the following modules:
- Customer database and contact management
- Sales pipeline and opportunity tracking
- Marketing campaign management and analytics
- Customer support ticketing system
- Reporting and business intelligence dashboard
- Mobile application (iOS and Android)
2.2 Technical Requirements
- Cloud-hosted on Developer's preferred infrastructure provider
- RESTful API architecture
- Responsive web interface
- Integration with Client's existing ERP system (SAP)
- Support for minimum 5,000 concurrent users
2.3 Deliverables
1. System architecture and design documents
2. Fully functional CRM application
3. API documentation
4. User training materials

5. Developer Personnel
5.1 Team Composition
The Developer shall provide the following team:
- 1 Project Manager
- 2 Senior Software Engineers
- 4 Software Developers
- 1 UX/UI Designer
- 2 QA Engineers
- 1 DevOps Engineer
5.2 Personnel Changes
The Developer may substitute personnel at its discretion provided that replacements have equivalent qualifications. The Client
shall be notified of personnel changes within 14 business days.
5.3 Subcontracting
The Developer may subcontract portions of the work to third parties without requiring prior approval from the Client. The
Developer remains responsible for the quality and timeliness of all subcontracted work.
6. Intellectual Property
6.1 Ownership
Upon full payment, the Client shall receive a perpetual, non-exclusive license to use the CRM system. The Developer retains
all intellectual property rights in the underlying code, frameworks, and tools developed during the project.
6.2 Developer Tools
The Developer may reuse any code, modules, libraries, or tools developed during this engagement in future projects for other
clients without restriction.
6.3 Open Source Components
The Developer may incorporate open source software components at its discretion. A list of open source components will be
provided upon project completion.

The Developer's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the amount paid by the Client in the 6 months preceding

9. Term and Termination
9.1 Term
This agreement shall remain in effect until all deliverables have been completed and the 12-month support period has ended.
9.2 Termination for Convenience
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. Upon termination, the Client shall pay for all work
completed to date.
9.3 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate immediately upon material breach that remains uncured for 60 days after written notice.
